About MEANOR

MEANOR is a young, mysterious artist whose music speaks louder than words. Her main hit "Paper Father" is an intimate confession about a father who gave gifts but not love. The "paper father" metaphor represents a fragile, nominal presence that cannot protect or support. Through her music, MEANOR turns personal pain into art, exploring the journey from longing to self-healing.
